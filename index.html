The first picture of the flower took me around 3 hours, it is an observational flower realism sketch, where I had to look at real flowers and replicate it using a pencil. The same goes to the 3rd pencil realism sketch of the flower, it was an art assignment that was given to me. The second flower is a pen line art, the whole flower was drawn using pen, no pencils were used. This also meant that there was no room for mistakes, all in all, this was one of the most fun art I have ever done The 4th drawing is a realism black panther pencil sketch, it took me 5 hours, on and off as there were many details on his armour. The final painting is a Chinese Ink Painting piece, would you believe it? I drew that whole thing when I was 12! The size of the drawing paper was bigger than my entire torso. Chinese ink painting is fun as it is very difficult to control the amount of water and paint, the paper for drawing is also very thin and it will break apart if too much water is used at once. This piece is the most challenging for me as a 12 year old. It is also my favourite piece.
